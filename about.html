Our Story

PROFESSIONAL DETAILING BUILT ON REAL EXPERIENCE

At SUN PRO, our work is built on real hands-on detailing experience — not just theory. We focus on practical, high-quality work that helps vehicles look cleaner, present better, and feel closer to new again.

Our team is supported by a senior manager with over 25 years of experience, providing technical guidance and helping maintain a strong standard of workmanship. I personally began this work in 2012 and have around 15 years of experience in car washing and detailing-focused work, including years of hands-on vehicle preparation and cosmetic improvement.

Over the years, I have worked on a large number of vehicles in detailing-oriented environments, and today I continue to carry out regular detailing work inside a Wangara Motors dealership setting. This gives us practical experience across many different vehicle conditions, helping us understand what each car needs and apply the right level of work for the best result.
