Why It Matters

WHY PROFESSIONAL DETAILING MATTERS

UV & Oxidation

Perth sun fades paint and causes oxidation. Protection helps slow damage before it becomes costly.

  • Fading & loss of gloss
  • Clear coat stress
  • Paint looks "tired"

Swirls & Micro Scratches

Auto washes and improper hand washing leave swirl marks. Paint correction restores clarity and depth.

  • Swirl mark reduction
  • Gloss & clarity restored
  • Deeper, cleaner reflection

Ceramic Protection

Ceramic coating adds a durable protective layer that resists chemicals and contamination.

  • Hydrophobic finish
  • Fallout resistance
  • Easier maintenance
